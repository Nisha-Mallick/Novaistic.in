Here we aim to help new developers and students discover impactful project ideas inspired by real-world problems. But this platform is not just for developers — it is also designed to assist the general public in finding solutions to the technical or practical problems they face in their daily lives.


If a solution is not readily available, these problems are highlighted as real-world problem statements, creating opportunities for students, developers, and innovators to work on meaningful projects that have real-life impact.

From technical issues to real-world challenges, we provide solutions to problems shared by anyone.

Contribute your ideas or solutions to help others and better display, even on mobile screens.

Turning the real world problems and public-submitted challenges into impactful project ideas.
